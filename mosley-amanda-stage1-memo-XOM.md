## Company Background & Objectives

Exxon Mobil Corp operates in the energy sector, focusing on upstream, downstream, and chemical operations worldwide. The objective of this analysis is to evaluate the company’s recent financial condition and performance. While financial statements present raw data, ratio analysis converts that information into actionable insights, allowing management to assess profitability, operational efficiency, leverage risk, and liquidity position more effectively.

---

## Approach

This analysis will focus on six key ratio categories:

- **Performance:** Evaluates overall returns (e.g., ROA, ROE) to measure effectiveness in generating profits.  
- **Profitability:** Assesses margins (gross, operating, net) to determine earnings strength.  
- **Efficiency:** Measures how well assets are utilized (asset turnover, inventory turnover).  
- **Leverage:** Examines financial risk and debt usage (debt-to-equity, interest coverage).  
- **Liquidity:** Assesses short-term financial stability (current ratio, quick ratio).  
- **DuPont Analysis:** Breaks down ROE into profit margin, asset turnover, and leverage for deeper insight.

Data will be sourced from SEC EDGAR (10-K filings), Exxon Mobil’s investor relations website, and financial platforms such as Yahoo Finance.
